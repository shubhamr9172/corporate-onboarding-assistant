# Employee Wellness & Health Benefits
[Category: Benefits]

## Topic: Medical Insurance & Coverage
All full-time employees are covered under our Group Medical Insurance Policy up to $10,000.
Cashless claims and e-cards can be requested and managed via the MediBuddy portal.

## Topic: Wellness & Office Allowances
We reimburse up to $50/month for gym memberships and fitness classes under Wellness Benefits.
Home office setups (ergonomic chairs, desks) have a one-time reimbursement allowance of up to $300.
Submit receipts in the Expense Reimbursement System (ERS) by the 25th of the month.
