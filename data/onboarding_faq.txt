[Category: IT Setup]
Topic: Laptop Requests & Credentials
New hires should submit a request for their IT hardware assets through the internal portal (ITSM) within their first day. The default package includes a MacBook Pro (16-inch or 14-inch depending on job role) or a Lenovo ThinkPad, along with an external monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Your login credentials (corporate email and domain account) will be generated by the systems team and sent to your personal email address.

Topic: VPN Access & MFA Security
To access corporate servers and internal tools, you must connect via the Corporate VPN. Install the GlobalProtect VPN client. You will be prompted to set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) via the Okta Verify app on your phone. All connections require MFA validation. If you experience lockouts, email helpdesk@company.com or raise a ticket at https://helpdesk.company.local.

Topic: Software Licenses & Access Requests
Requests for specialized software licenses (e.g., Adobe Creative Cloud, JetBrains IDEs, AWS Sandboxes, Salesforce) must be submitted via the Access Management System (AMS) and approved by your reporting manager. Access to Slack channels and Google Workspace is configured automatically during HR provisioning.

[Category: Policy]
Topic: Working Hours & Attendance
Our core working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday. We support flexible working arrangements, and team members can coordinate core hours with their respective managers. Attendance and time tracking should be logged daily in the HR Portal (HRMS) under the Timesheet section.

Topic: Leave Policy (Sick, Casual, Earned Leaves)
Employees are entitled to 24 days of paid leave per calendar year, which includes:
- 12 days of Earned Leave (EL), accrued monthly.
- 6 days of Sick Leave (SL).
- 6 days of Casual Leave (CL).
Maternity leave is 26 weeks, and paternity leave is 2 weeks. All leave requests must be submitted in the HRMS at least 3 days in advance (except for emergency sick leaves) and approved by your manager.

Topic: Code of Conduct & Compliance
Employees must strictly adhere to the Business Code of Conduct, keeping all client details and intellectual property confidential. Complete the mandatory security compliance training within your first 15 days on the LMS portal. Failure to complete compliance courses may result in access suspension.

[Category: Benefits]
Topic: Medical Insurance & Health Benefits
All full-time employees are covered under the Group Medical Insurance Policy. The policy provides cover of up to $10,000 (or equivalent local currency) for the employee, spouse, and up to two dependent children. Group Term Life Insurance is also provided. Medical claims and cashless cards can be managed via the MediBuddy portal.

Topic: Allowances & Reimbursements
We provide several employee reimbursements:
- Gym & Wellness: Up to $50 per month for gym memberships or fitness classes.
- Home Office Setup: A one-time allowance of up to $300 for ergonomic office furniture (chair, desk).
- Internet & Mobile: Up to $30 per month for home broadband and mobile billing.
Submit bills under the 'Claims' tab in the Expense Reimbursement System (ERS) by the 25th of each month.

[Category: Payroll]
Topic: Salary Credit & Payroll Cycle
Salary is processed monthly and credited directly to your registered corporate bank account on the last working day of each month. Payslips can be viewed and downloaded from the Payroll Portal (AdpPay) by the 3rd of the following month.

Topic: Bank Account & Tax Declaration
Provide your bank account details, PAN/Tax ID, and tax declaration documents (such as investment proofs for tax deductions) on the AdpPay portal during your first week. Tax declarations help calculate withholding tax correctly. Missing declarations will result in maximum tax deductions.

New hires report directly to their assigned engineering manager or team lead. You will receive an invitation to a 1-on-1 weekly sync with your manager. The organization chart is available on the BambooHR directory under the Org Chart tab, where you can see department reporting lines.
