# Corporate Onboarding: Application Access Guide

This document outlines the standard operating procedures for requesting and managing access to corporate software applications and platforms.

## 1. Overview of Application Access
All corporate applications are governed by identity and access management (IAM) policies. Standard access is provisioned automatically based on your department, role, and job title on day one. Special or additional application access must be requested manually.

## 2. Standard Applications (Auto-Provisioned)
Upon completion of HR onboarding and Active Directory account activation, the following applications are automatically provisioned within 24 hours:
- **Corporate Email & Calendar**: Microsoft Outlook / Office 365
- **Collaboration & Chat**: Slack (joined to department channels automatically)
- **Document Management**: Confluence and SharePoint (Read-only access to corporate spaces)
- **HR & Payroll Portal**: Workday (for benefits, time tracking, and profile details)

## 3. Specialized Applications (Manual Request Required)
For applications that are not auto-provisioned, requests must be submitted through our service portal:

### A. Jira & Confluence (Write/Admin Access)
- **Tool Purpose**: Task tracking, project management, and wiki editing.
- **Request Platform**: ServiceNow Portal -> Request Catalog -> "Jira & Confluence Access Request"
- **Required Details**: 
  - Project/Space keys you need access to (e.g., `DEVOPS`, `HR-ONB`).
  - Access Level: User, Contributor, or Project Administrator.
- **Approval Flow**: Line Manager approval is required. Project Administrator approval is required for admin rights.

### B. Salesforce (CRM Access)
- **Tool Purpose**: Sales, customer support, and account management.
- **Request Platform**: SailPoint Identity Portal -> Request Access -> "Salesforce User Profile"
- **Required Details**: 
  - Salesforce Profile Role (e.g., Sales Rep, Support Agent, Read-Only).
  - Region/Territory scope.
- **Approval Flow**: Line Manager approval followed by Salesforce Business Owner sign-off.

### C. ServiceNow (IT Service Management)
- **Tool Purpose**: Ticketing, asset management, and change requests.
- **Request Platform**: ServiceNow -> Self-Service -> "Request Access for ITIL Role"
- **Required Details**: 
  - Specific assignment group you belong to.
- **Approval Flow**: Manager approval is required.

## 4. Submission and Troubleshooting
If you encounter any issues accessing provisioned applications, or if your request is delayed:
1. Verify your network connection or ensure you are connected to the Corporate VPN.
2. Check the status of your request in the **ServiceNow Portal -> My Requests**.
3. Contact the IT Helpdesk at `it-support@corporate.com` or via the Slack channel `#it-helpdesk`.
